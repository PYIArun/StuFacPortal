About StuFac

Welcome to the Student-Faculty Communication Portal. The portal aims to provide a user-friendly interface where faculty members can post important announcements related to courses, events, and deadlines. Additionally, the portal allows faculty members to share educational resources such as lecture notes, study materials, and presentations with their students. Stay in the loop with important announcements and find helpful resources uploaded by teachers. Join discussions, ask questions, and even chat with teachers during office hours. Our goal is to make learning and communication simple and effective for everyone. Join us and be part of this collaborative learning journey!
